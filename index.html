Zomb'es

Zomb’es is a British themed zombie round based game taking you on a tour through the London underground. Battle through waves of British themed zombies or complete the side quest to eradicate the zombies from England! The game is set in a post-apocalyptic dystopia. You can choose whether to survive the rounds or complete the side quest allowing for different replay ability.

Is it Worth it Games

Is it Worth it Games is a game development studio based in Newcastle-under-Lyme. Due to our recent success with JDM Time Trial we decided to make a zombie survival game. Zomb'es is a round based zombie survival game where the player can choose to either survive the round or complete the side quest.

Platforms

Zomb'es will be released on the following platforms: Xbox, PlayStation and PC. £30.00 all platforms.
